Defense & Electronics

The importance of being self-reliant in the defence sector needs no emphasis as it is not only of critical strategic and national importance but also an essential component of the national power. The country has made significant progress in achieving this goal. However, much remains to be done.

Someone cannot argue that technology in healthcare is a new thing. We use medical technology from the most severe case to the simplest medical case, like breaking a bone.
